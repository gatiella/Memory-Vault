🔐 Privacy Policy

Memory Vault

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Memory Vault is a personal notes and to-do application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide the app's core functionality:

  • Account information — your email address and display name when you sign in with Google or email/password via Firebase Authentication.
  • Notes and to-dos — the content you create inside the app, stored securely in Firebase Firestore under your unique user ID.
  • Google Drive data — if you use the Google Drive backup feature, your notes and to-dos are saved to a folder called MyNotes in your own Google Drive account. We only access files created by this app (drive.file scope).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and keep your data private to your account.
  • To store, sync, and display your notes and to-dos across devices.
  • To back up your data to your personal Google Drive when enabled.
We do not sell, share, or monetize your personal data in any way. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), protected by Firebase Security Rules that ensure only you can access your own data. All communication is encrypted in transit via HTTPS/TLS.

Google Drive backups are stored in your own Google account and are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Biometric Data

Memory Vault uses your device's biometric authentication (fingerprint or face ID) as an optional app lock. Biometric data is processed entirely on your device by the operating system and is never transmitted to us or any server.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and database
  • Google Sign-In & Google Drive — login and backup

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete your notes and to-dos at any time from within the app. To request full account and data deletion, contact us at the email below and we will delete your data from Firebase within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Memory Vault is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
